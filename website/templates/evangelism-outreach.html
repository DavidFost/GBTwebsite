Evangelism & Outreach

Our Impact

Welcome to our Evangelism and Outreach Ministry, where we are passionate about sharing the love of Christ and making a positive impact in our community and beyond. We invite you to join us in reaching out to those in need, spreading hope, and making a difference in people's lives. Through various outreach initiatives, missions, and community service projects, we strive to demonstrate God's love in tangible ways. Whether it's feeding the hungry, supporting local organizations, or sharing the message of salvation, there's a place for you to get involved and be part of something meaningful. Together, let's extend a hand of compassion, bring light to the darkness, and be agents of transformation in our world. Join our Evangelism and Outreach Ministry and discover the joy of serving others while growing in your own faith. Let's make a lasting impact together!
